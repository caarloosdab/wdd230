Site Plan: Mexico's Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The name of this chamber site is Mexico's Chamber of Commerce. This name was selected because it represents the local business community and highlights its purpose to foster economic growth in Mexico.

Site Purpose

This site aims to promote local businesses and provide a resource for the community to stay informed about economic and social development. Additionally, the site will facilitate networking opportunities for business owners and potential investors.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events will foster business-to-business connections?
  • Where can I find information about the chamber's leadership and board members?
  • How can I access recent economic data and trends for the town?

Color Schema

The primary color for the site will be a deep blue (#004080), used for headings and navigation to convey professionalism. The secondary color will be light gray (#f0f0f0), used for background sections to create contrast and readability.

Typography

The site will use 'Roboto' for body text due to its clean and modern appearance, while 'Merriweather' will be used for headings to create a strong and traditional look.
